# BMW Sales Performance Analysis Report

## 1. Executive Summary

This report provides an analysis of BMW's sales performance from 2020 to 2024, examining both overall global trends and regional contributions. The data indicates a generally positive growth trajectory over the period, with a notable peak in 2022 and a subsequent dip in 2023, followed by a strong recovery in 2024. Regionally, Asia and Europe emerged as the leading sales contributors in 2024, while most regions showed fluctuations aligned with the overall global trend.

## 2. Overall Sales Trend

Global BMW sales experienced fluctuations between 2020 and 2024. Sales steadily grew from 16.3 million units in 2020 to a peak of 17.9 million units in 2022. A subsequent decline was observed in 2023, with sales dropping to 16.2 million units. However, the company demonstrated a strong recovery in 2024, reaching 17.5 million units, nearing its 2022 peak performance.

![Sales by Year](../reports/sales_by_year.png)

*   **2020:** 16,310,843 units
*   **2021:** 16,884,666 units (3.5% increase from 2020)
*   **2022:** 17,920,946 units (6.1% increase from 2021) - **Peak Sales**
*   **2023:** 16,268,654 units (9.3% decrease from 2022)
*   **2024:** 17,527,854 units (7.7% increase from 2023) - **Strong Recovery**

The data highlights the resilience and recovery capabilities of BMW's global sales operations following a challenging year in 2023.

## 3. Regional Sales Performance

Regional sales data reveals varying contributions and trends across different markets. While most regions mirrored the overall global trend of peaking in 2022 and recovering in 2024 after a 2023 dip, the magnitudes and specific year-over-year changes differed.

![Sales by Region and Year](../reports/sales_by_region_year.png)

**Key Regional Observations (2020-2024):**

*   **Asia:** Demonstrated strong growth over the period, starting at 2.4 million units in 2020 and reaching the highest regional sales of 3.08 million units in 2024. This region consistently shows robust performance and is a key growth driver.
*   **Europe:** Maintained a strong performance, consistently being one of the top-contributing regions. Sales grew from 2.8 million in 2020 to 3.03 million in 2024, ranking as the second-highest region in the latest year.
*   **Middle East:** Showed relatively stable sales figures, hovering around 2.9 million units throughout the period, with a slight dip in 2023 and recovery in 2024.
*   **North America:** Experienced a peak in 2022 at 3.01 million units, but unlike other regions, sales slightly declined in 2023 and further in 2024 to 2.86 million units, showing a different recovery trajectory compared to the overall global trend.
*   **Africa and South America:** These regions generally recorded lower sales volumes compared to others but followed the global trend of a 2022 peak, 2023 dip, and 2024 recovery. Africa recovered to 2.8 million units in 2024, while South America also recovered to 2.8 million units.

In 2024, **Asia** led with 3,080,909 units, closely followed by **Europe** with 3,033,044 units. **South America** and **Africa** were the lowest contributors in 2024, with 2,803,029 and 2,805,506 units respectively, although they showed a good recovery from their 2023 lows.
